Medieval Manuscripts: Materials and Techniques

In the summer of 2022, six Pitt-Greensburg students, three professors, and a host of librarians assembled to study a collection of medieval manuscripts in depth. This page represents some of our work.

We would like to express our gratitude:

  • To Les Enluminures, for their generosity in creating the Manuscripts in the Curriculum program that allowed us to borrow these manuscripts and study them in person;
  • To the staff of the University Library System, and especially the Archives and Special Collections, for going above and beyond for us throughout this process;
  • And to the Samuel H. Kress Foundation and the Gladys Krieble Delmas Foundation for the funding that made it all possible.
